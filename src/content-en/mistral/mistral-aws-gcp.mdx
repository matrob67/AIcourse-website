## Mistral on AWS and GCP: Everywhere, Not Just Azure

Mistral isn't exclusive to Azure. The models are also available on **Amazon Bedrock** and **Google Cloud Model Garden**. If your organization is already invested in one of these clouds, you can use Mistral without changing infrastructure.

The idea is simple: no matter your cloud provider, Mistral is accessible. You don't have to migrate to Azure to benefit from European AI.

### AWS Bedrock: Mistral in the Amazon Ecosystem

Amazon Bedrock is AWS's foundation model platform. Think of it as a catalog of AI models you can consume via a managed API, without managing servers.

**Available Mistral models**:
- Mistral Large (the flagship)
- Mistral Small (fast and economical)
- Mixtral 8x7B (the MoE pioneer)
- Mistral 7B (the founding model)

All accessible via a managed API: no servers to provision, no infrastructure to manage.

**Native AWS integrations**: this is where Bedrock shines. Mistral integrates into the entire Amazon ecosystem:
- **Lambda**: invoke Mistral from serverless functions, perfect for event-driven architectures
- **S3**: input and output data flows directly from/to S3
- **SageMaker**: for advanced model fine-tuning or evaluation
- **IAM**: granular access control, you can authorize each model separately
- **CloudWatch**: monitoring of calls, latencies, and costs

**Bedrock Knowledge Bases**: this is AWS's turnkey RAG. Upload your documents, Bedrock chunks them, vectorizes them, and indexes them automatically. Your queries are then enriched with relevant context, without you having to build an ETL pipeline.

<Callout type="info" title="Bedrock Knowledge Bases: RAG Without the Effort">
If you have proprietary documents and want to do RAG, Bedrock Knowledge Bases automates the entire pipeline: ingestion, chunking, vectorization, indexing. It's production RAG without writing a complex ETL (Extract-Transform-Load) pipeline. Ideal for teams that want fast results.
</Callout>

**Bedrock Guardrails**: content filtering, PII (Personally Identifiable Information) detection, deny lists. These protections layer on top of Mistral's native guardrails for a double security layer.

### Google Cloud Model Garden: Mistral in the Google Ecosystem

Google Cloud offers Mistral via Model Garden, with a particular emphasis on integration with **Vertex AI** (their ML platform).

**Available models**:
- Mistral Large (managed API)
- Mistral Small (managed API)
- Mixtral 8x7B (managed API + deployment on GKE)
- Open-weight models (deployable on your own GKE clusters)

**Native GCP integrations**:
- **Vertex AI**: fine-tuning, evaluation, custom deployment in a unified environment
- **BigQuery**: call Mistral **directly from SQL queries**. This is the killer feature.
- **Cloud Functions / Cloud Run**: serverless deployments
- **GKE**: deploy open-weight models on your Kubernetes clusters for total control

**BigQuery ML + Mistral: GCP's real killer feature**. Imagine: you have 10 million customer comments in BigQuery. With a single SQL query, you classify them by sentiment, extract recurring themes, and summarize trends. No Python script, no ETL pipeline, no inference server to manage. Just SQL.

<Callout type="tip" title={"BigQuery + Mistral: AI in SQL"}>
You have millions of rows of text data in BigQuery. A single SQL query with Mistral enriches, classifies, and summarizes them. No application code, no data pipeline. If your data is already in BigQuery, this is the simplest way to apply AI at scale.
</Callout>

### How to Choose Your Cloud for Mistral

The choice depends primarily on your existing ecosystem:

**Are you Microsoft-first?** Go with Azure. The partnership with Mistral is the deepest there, and integration with Office 365, Azure AD, and the Microsoft ecosystem is native.

**Are you AWS-first?** Bedrock is your natural choice. Knowledge Bases for effortless RAG, solid Lambda/SageMaker integration, and the AWS ecosystem you already know.

**Are you GCP-first?** Model Garden + Vertex AI. If your data is in BigQuery, the ability to call Mistral directly from SQL is a unique and powerful advantage.

**Are you multi-cloud or cloud-agnostic?** Use Mistral's La Plateforme directly. It's neutral with respect to clouds, often cheaper (no intermediary margin), and your code stays portable.

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Out for Cloud Lock-in">
The more you integrate Mistral with cloud-specific services (Bedrock guardrails, Knowledge Bases, BigQuery ML), the more you're tied to that cloud. If portability is a strategic priority, stick with Mistral's La Plateforme directly and cloud-agnostic tools (LangChain, LlamaIndex, open-source vector databases).
</Callout>

### Pricing: The Cost of the Intermediary

Clouds generally charge a **margin** over Mistral's direct La Plateforme rates. You pay more per token.

But several factors offset this extra cost:
- **Centralized billing**: a single invoice for all your cloud consumption
- **Ecosystem integration**: RAG, guardrails, monitoring without additional tools
- **Cloud support**: guaranteed SLA, technical support
- **Existing credits**: if your organization has negotiated cloud credits, use them

For many organizations, the operational simplification is worth the per-token premium.
