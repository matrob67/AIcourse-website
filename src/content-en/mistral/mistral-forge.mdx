## Mistral Forge: Your AI, Your Data, Your Model

**Mistral Forge** is Mistral's most ambitious product. Announced at NVIDIA GTC in March 2026, it goes far beyond simple fine-tuning.

The idea: instead of adjusting a generic model with a few examples, you build a **complete model** massively trained on your proprietary data. It's the difference between giving a job description to a generalist and training a domain expert for months.

### The Problem with Classic Fine-tuning

A model fine-tuned on a few thousand examples will improve its style, response format, and ability to follow your conventions. That's useful. But it doesn't **fundamentally understand** your domain if it was never deeply exposed to it.

Let's take a concrete example: a Mistral model fine-tuned on 5,000 legal contracts will produce better legal responses than a base model. But a model trained on **1 million** contracts, case law decisions, legal scholarship, and regulatory texts will have integrated law into its weights at a fundamental level. It will "think" legal, instead of just "imitating" legal.

**Forge** = deep and massive training, not lightweight fine-tuning.

<Callout type="info" title="Continued Pre-training: The Underlying Technique">
Forge relies on "continued pre-training": you take an already pre-trained model (like Mistral Large) and train it **further** on your proprietary corpus, using the same techniques as the initial training. The result: the model integrates your knowledge into its weights, instead of receiving it at each request via the context. It's permanent knowledge, not temporary.
</Callout>

### Forge's Capabilities

**Continued Pre-training**: train the model on your complete corpus (internal documents, knowledge base, communication history, technical documentation). Your knowledge is anchored in the model's weights, not in a temporary prompt.

**Advanced Fine-tuning**: beyond classic SFT (Supervised Fine-Tuning), Forge offers multi-task learning, custom RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback), and alignment on your business's specific preferences.

**Adapted Architecture**: adjust the model size, number of MoE experts, and context length to the constraints of your use case. A smaller but hyper-specialized model can outperform a giant generalist model on your domain.

**Sovereign Data Management**: complete data isolation, end-to-end encryption, traceability of the provenance of each training data point. Your data never leaves European territory.

**Dedicated Infrastructure**: the GPUs used for your training are dedicated to your organization, with guaranteed performance. Deployment options include Mistral Compute (sovereign datacenter) or OUTSCALE (SecNumCloud).

<Callout type="tip" title="Data Sovereignty: The Crucial Point">
For regulated industries (finance, healthcare, defense), Forge combined with sovereign infrastructure is the ideal solution: your proprietary data stays in Europe, your custom model is under your total control, and no US dependency intervenes in the chain.
</Callout>



### Use Cases: Where Forge Makes the Difference

**Financial services**: a model specialized in risk analysis, regulatory compliance, and fraud detection, trained on 10 years of historical banking data. It knows your institution's patterns, not those of a generic bank.

**Healthcare and pharma**: a model that understands the medical terminology specific to your specialty, your facility's care protocols, drug interactions, and the scientific literature in your field. Precision is critical when lives are at stake.

**Manufacturing**: a model expert in predictive maintenance, production process optimization, and technical support. It knows your machines, your procedures, your failure histories.

**Legal**: a model trained on case law in your area of expertise, your firm's contract templates, and applicable regulations. It produces legal analyses in your style and according to your standards.

### The Typical Forge Project Process

1. **Discovery**: analysis of your needs, identification of priority use cases, technical feasibility verification
2. **Data Preparation**: collection, cleaning, and structuring of your proprietary corpora (this is often the longest step)
3. **Training**: continued pre-training then fine-tuning on dedicated infrastructure
4. **Evaluation**: testing on custom benchmarks and real cases from your domain
5. **Deployment**: production launch via La Plateforme, Mistral Compute, or on-premise
6. **Iteration**: continuous improvement based on real usage and user feedback

<Callout type="warning" title="Forge Is for Large Organizations">
Forge is a premium product aimed at large organizations. The investments are significant: time (data preparation and training), data (you need a proprietary corpus of sufficient quality and volume), and budget (compute for continued pre-training is expensive). Seriously evaluate the return on investment before getting started.
</Callout>

### Competitive Advantage Over Alternatives

Compared to OpenAI Custom Models and Google Custom Training, Mistral Forge has three distinctive strengths:

1. **European sovereignty**: your training data and resulting model stay in Europe, under European jurisdiction
2. **MoE architecture**: the superior efficiency of MoE enables more performant models at a lower inference cost
3. **Weight ownership**: you own your model's weights and can deploy it on-premise, without ongoing dependency on a cloud provider
