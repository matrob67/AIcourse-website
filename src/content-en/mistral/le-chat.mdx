## Le Chat: The European Alternative to ChatGPT

You know ChatGPT? Imagine the same thing, but **European**, with a **more attractive price**, and your data stored in Europe. That's Le Chat.

Available at chat.mistral.ai, it's Mistral's consumer-facing interface. You talk to an AI without touching a single line of code. It's the product your manager, your accountant, or your intern can use without any technical training.



### Three Tiers: From Free to Corporate

**Free**: free, no credit card required
- Access to Mistral Small and Medium
- Soft limits on the number of messages per day
- Basic web search
- Saved conversation history
- iOS and Android mobile apps

This is the ideal tier for the curious, students, and anyone who wants to explore conversational AI without commitment.

**Pro (14.99 euros/month)**: the best value on the market
- **Mistral Large 3**: Mistral's most powerful model
- **Magistral**: the advanced reasoning model for complex tasks
- High message limits
- Canvas (collaborative workspace, see below)
- Custom agents
- Priority service during peak loads

This is the tier for professionals who want AI power in their daily workflow: writing, analysis, brainstorming, coding.

**Enterprise**: everything Pro offers, plus corporate features
- User and team administration
- SSO (Single Sign-On: enterprise authentication via SAML/OIDC)
- Customizable data privacy and retention policies
- Dedicated support with guaranteed SLA
- Enhanced GDPR compliance

For organizations deploying AI at scale.

<Callout type="info" title="Pricing: Mistral vs OpenAI vs Anthropic">
Le Chat Pro costs 14.99 euros per month, compared to 20 dollars (about 18 euros) for ChatGPT Plus and Claude Pro. With European data sovereignty as a bonus, the value proposition is hard to beat.
</Callout>

### The Features That Make the Difference

**Real-time web search**: Le Chat can search the internet for you, in real time, and cite its sources. Essential for current events, prices, evolving information, or any topic after the model's knowledge cutoff date.

**Canvas: the collaborative workspace**: instead of the classic copy-paste back-and-forth between the chat and your editor, Canvas is an **integrated workspace** directly in the interface. You write, Le Chat rewrites or improves, you iterate together on the same document.

Concrete Canvas use cases:
- Writing an article: Le Chat proposes a first draft, you refine, it rephrases
- Generating code: Le Chat codes, you test and adjust directly
- Creating structured documents: reports, presentations, specifications

<Callout type="tip" title="Canvas vs Classic Chat: Two Complementary Modes">
Normal chat is made for quick Q&A: "What's the capital of Burkina Faso?" Canvas is made for iterative work on a document or project. Both modes complement each other depending on the need.
</Callout>

**Custom agents**: create your own tailor-made AI, configured for your specific use.
- Define custom system instructions (role, tone, constraints)
- Attach reference documents (PDFs, notes, knowledge bases)
- Configure the tools the agent can use
- Share the agent with your colleagues

Concrete example: create a "French legal assistant" agent that has access to your standard contract database, knows French labor law, and responds in a formal style. Your colleagues use it like an in-house expert.

**Image understanding**: thanks to the multimodal capabilities of Pixtral and Mistral Large 3, Le Chat understands photos and images.
- Describe the content of an image
- Extract text (intelligent OCR)
- Analyze a chart or diagram
- Solve a math problem from a photo of the problem statement

### Privacy: The European Advantage

This is Le Chat's killer argument against ChatGPT:

- **No training on your conversations**: by default, Mistral doesn't use your exchanges to train its models. With OpenAI, you have to explicitly opt out of this.
- **Servers in Europe**: your data physically stays on European territory, GDPR applies natively
- **Deletion on request**: you can delete your data whenever you want

For companies sensitive to data privacy, this is a decisive argument.

<Callout type="warning" title={"No Native Image Generation"}>
ChatGPT integrates DALL-E for image generation. Le Chat, as of today, doesn't have a built-in image generator. If image creation is a critical need for you, this is a limitation to keep in mind.
</Callout>
